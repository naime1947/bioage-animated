Your BioAge and your health are not done!

The following lifestyle recommendations have been proven tohelp people like you to optimize their blood sugar, weight and blood pressure.

Our advice on what you can do to improve your health:

Regular Exercise

Exercise helps the body use insulin more efficiently, which can lead to better blood sugar control. It can also lower blood pressure and help to achieve or maintain a healthy weight.

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week.

Regular Exercise

Eat Healthy

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in fiber, healthy fats, and lean proteins can help regulate blood sugar levels. Avoid foods high in sugar and refined carbohydrates, which can cause spikes in blood sugar levels.

Dehydration can cause blood sugar levels to rise, so it's important to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day. Drink plenty of water throughout the day to help curb hunger and promote good digestion.

Certain supplements, like chromium and magnesium, may help regulate blood sugar levels. Talk to your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.

Monitor your portion sizes: Use smaller plates and pay attention to serving sizes to prevent overeating

Eat Healthy

Manage Stress

High levels of stress can cause blood sugar levels, weight and blood pressure to rise, so it's important to manage stress levels.

Try to manage stress through relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.

Manage Stress

Quit Smoking

Smoking can raise blood pressure and increase the risk of heart disease. 

Speak to your doctor for guidance and assistance with quitting smoking.

Quit Smoking
